The Problem

Our booking tool is broken.

People book desks and never show up. Teams can't coordinate. Nobody trusts the data.

~20%
of booked desks are

❓

where your team sits
before you come in
📋
Office managers flying
blind on utilisation
with no actionable data
The Solution

Three things we fixed.

🚫

Kill the no-show

Auto-release desks after a grace period. No check-in = desk freed for the next person on the waitlist. No manual chasing.

🗺️

Team coordination

See where colleagues are sitting before you commute in. Managers can block-book team zones and teammates claim from a shared pool.

📊

Honest data

Booked ≠ occupied. Track check-ins separately to see real utilisation, peak days, no-show rates, and room hoarding patterns.

With More Time

What we'd build next.

🔲

Walk-up QR Booking

Scan the QR code on any free desk to book it on the spot — no navigation, no searching. Walk in, find a desk you like, scan and it's yours.

Zero-friction last-minute booking
📧

Managed Invites + 48h Claim Window

Manager reserves a block of desks and sends email or push invites to team members and guests. Each person gets 48 hours to claim their spot before it auto-releases.

Guaranteed spots for key collaboration days
📅

Calendar Integration

Sync desk bookings with Google Calendar and Outlook. See your desk next to your meetings — and get smart nudges to book on days you already have in-office events.

One view for your whole day
🤖

Smarter AI with Claude API

Replace the local resolver with Claude API for richer intent understanding, multi-turn conversations, and booking suggestions that learn your patterns over time.

Booking that improves with use
